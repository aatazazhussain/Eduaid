About EduAid

Empowering dreams through access to quality education

Our Mission

EduAid is dedicated to transforming lives by making higher education accessible to every deserving student in Pakistan. We aim to remove financial barriers through Study Now Pay Later (SNPL) programs and strategic partnerships.

Why We Exist

Inspired by the story of Aleena Nadeem, a bright student who overcame financial hardship, EduAid was created to give thousands like her a fair shot at their dreams. Her resilience drives our vision.

Our Core Values

  • Access: Every student deserves education regardless of income.
  • Integrity: We are transparent, fair, and accountable in all we do.
  • Impact: We strive to create lasting change, not just short-term relief.

"Education is not a privilege, it's a right. We are here to make sure no one is left behind."

— EduAid Foundation
