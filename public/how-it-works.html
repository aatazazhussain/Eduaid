How EduAid Works

From signup to scholarship grant — your journey made simple.

Step-by-Step Process

1. Create Your Profile

Sign up and build a complete student profile including academic background and financial need details.

2. Explore Scholarships

Browse a list of verified scholarships from top Pakistani universities and private partners.

3. Check Eligibility

Use our smart filters to find scholarships that match your academic, financial, and regional criteria.

4. Submit Application

Upload required documents (transcripts, income proof, etc.) directly through our secure portal.

5. Get Funded

If approved, you receive confirmation and your tuition is paid directly to your university.
